IV. THE HALF-LIFE OF TRUST
Trust decays, but it doesn't disappear.
A verification from 2 years ago still matters—it proves you were once reliable. But recent behavior is predictive of future behavior. Reputation must reflect this.
Kinetix Reputation Score (KRS) uses a half-life model:
* Recent verifications weighted heavily
* Older attestations visible but discounted over time
* Continuous verification maintains high scores
* Dormant agents see scores naturally decay
Why this matters: Agents can't rest on old laurels. Reputation is earned continuously, not once.
