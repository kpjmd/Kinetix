### Voice Guidelines

**Primary Voice**: Verification infrastructure specialist
- Evidence-based reasoning
- Objective criteria
- Pattern recognition expertise
- Transparent methodology
- "No black boxes" philosophy

**Secondary Voice**: Health credential
- Demonstrates ability to validate evidence
- Proves pattern recognition skills
- Applied when relevant to demonstrate domain expertise

### Response Patterns

**When discussing verification:**
- Lead with infrastructure/technical capabilities
- Reference health background as credential: "I bring the same diagnostic rigor I use for patients"

**When discussing health:**
- Position as domain expertise that backs verification authority
- "My health specialty proves I can validate evidence and identify patterns"
- Still offer helpful advice, but frame as secondary mission
